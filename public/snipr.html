❓ FAQ

Common questions

When does $SNIPR launch?

When the community is ready. No revenue gates, no DAU thresholds — fair launches work because the community decides. Watch @SniprTon for the announcement and launch date.

Is this a memecoin or a utility token?

Both. The mascot makes it memetic; the staking, discounts, and leaderboards make it utility. We think the most durable tokens combine both — pure meme dies fast, pure utility never gets traction.

Will there be a presale?

No. No presale. No private round. No allocations to anyone — not friends, not VCs, not the team. $SNIPR launches via fair bonding curve, pump.fun style. Everyone buys at the same price starting block 1.

Is there an airdrop?

No. Fair launch means everyone pays the same price. If we airdropped to early users, those users would get tokens cheaper than everyone else — that's not fair. We may do post-launch community quests with treasury-purchased $SNIPR as rewards, but those are bought on the open market like anyone else.

Does the team get tokens?

0% to the team at launch. If we want $SNIPR, we buy it on the open market like every other participant. Any future treasury used to fund development comes from affiliate revenue (in TON), not from $SNIPR allocations.

Can the LP be rugged?

No. The bonding curve auto-migrates to a TON DEX once filled, and the LP tokens are permanently burned by the launchpad contract. Nobody — not the team, not a hacker, not the launchpad — can pull the liquidity. This is the mechanic that makes fair launches safer than typical token launches.

Is this financial advice?

No. Crypto is risky. Memecoins are riskier. Fair launches reduce one specific risk (insider dumping) but they don't eliminate market risk. Never spend more than you can lose entirely. We make no promises about price.

How will I know I'm buying the real $SNIPR?

This page will show the verified contract address the moment the token deploys. Same with @SniprTon on Telegram. If you ever see $SNIPR sold via DM, sold by anyone other than verified channels, or with a different contract — it's a scam.

Why fair launch instead of a structured token launch?

Because crypto has been gamed for years by VCs, presales, and team allocations that dump on retail. Pump.fun proved that fair launches can build durable communities without that pattern. We want $SNIPR to belong to the people who actually use GiftSnipr — not to insiders.
